Pattern Spotting

The system looks for emotional patterns in text ‚Äî like certain words, phrases, or styles ‚Äî that usually connect to feelings like joy, frustration, excitement, or sadness. It doesn't just look at one word but tries to read the vibe of a whole sentence or paragraph.

Emotion Mapping

Instead of saying "this is positive" or "this is negative," EmotionScore tries to figure out what kind of emotion it is, and how strong it feels. It maps text on multiple levels ‚Äî like whether something feels calm, anxious, warm, tense ‚Äî kind of like giving it emotional coordinates.

Learning Across Cultures

People express emotions differently depending on where they're from or what language they use. This project is slowly learning how to pick up on those differences, so it doesn't treat all language the same. It's still very early, but the goal is to make it fair and flexible.
